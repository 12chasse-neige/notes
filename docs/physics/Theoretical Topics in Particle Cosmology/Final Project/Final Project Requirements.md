# Final Project Requirements

**Course:** Theoretical Topics in Particle Cosmology 
**Semester:** Spring 2026

For the final project, write a term paper on **one** of the following topics.



## Topic 1: Nonminimal interactions of massless particles

Study self-interactions and mutual interactions of massless particles with nonzero spin, going beyond minimal couplings.

1. Begin at the three-point level and find every possible nonzero coupling among three massless particles of either the same or different spins.
2. Continue to the four-point level and use the consistency conditions introduced in the lectures to constrain these couplings.
3. Clearly summarize all “no-go” and “yes-go” results.



## Topic 2: Mercury precession

Fill in as many missing steps from Lecture 10 as possible and write a self-contained account of how to compute Mercury’s precession rate from scattering amplitudes.



## Submission requirements

- The paper may be written in English or Chinese, but English is strongly encouraged.
- Writing the paper in LaTeX is strongly recommended.
- There is no fixed length limit. Choose an appropriate length, but the paper is expected to be substantial.
- Do not repeat material already covered in the lecture notes.
- Do not make the paper unnecessarily long.
- Cite all previous work on which the project relies.
- Discussion with other people and with AI is allowed, but all derivations and writing must be your own.
- Directly generating the paper with AI is unacceptable.
- If a discussion with another person or AI benefits the project, acknowledge that assistance appropriately.
